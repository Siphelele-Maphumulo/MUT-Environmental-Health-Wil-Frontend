GUIDELINES TO INDUSTRY MENTORS

The Department of Environmental Health at Mangosuthu University of Technology would like to thank you for your continued support for Work Integrated Learning (WIL) for students studying for the Environmental Health qualification. We greatly value the time and the impartation of practical professional knowledge to our students.

Ideally, EHP/Industry mentors undertake the following activities during WIL placement:

  • check_circle Responsible for the pre-agreed work performed by the student along with the level of practice.
  • check_circle Negotiate the work duties and learning goals with the academic supervisor and in some cases, with the student.
  • check_circle Arrange a workplace induction for the student.
  • check_circle Provide ongoing supervision of the student, preferably through regular timetabled meetings, with written outcomes forming a documentary record of this journey.
  • check_circle Provide constructive and ongoing feedback on the student's performance in the workplace.
  • check_circle Provide the student with the opportunity to reconcile workplace theory and practice and the chance to reflect on workplace activities.
  • check_circle Verify whether learning goals have been met and evaluate and report on student outcomes.
